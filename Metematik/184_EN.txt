Short division. ACTIVITY.
Properties of numbers. Materials: Activity sheet. Number of participants: 2-3. Divisible by 3. Even number.
You must place the numbers in the box in one of the areas formed by the three circles on the activity sheet.
We help you with some figures.
The number 9 is divisible by 3 but is not divisible by 5.
It is also not an even number.
The number 18 is an even number that is divisible by 3.
The number 45 is divisible by both 3 and 5. But it is not an even number.
Take turns writing in one number at a time until all 18 numbers are placed somewhere in the circles. Make sure you agree.
Compare your results with another group.
