Multiplication by 10, 100 and 1000.
MULTIPLICATION WITH POSITIVE INTEGER NUMBERS.
Multiplying positive integers by 10, 100 or 1000 is easy.
The product is 10, 100 or 1000 times as large.
This means that all numbers in the product get values that are 10, 100 or 1000 times larger.
You can think like this. 5 tens are worth SEK 50.
5 hundred notes are worth SEK 500. 5 thousand notes are worth SEK 5,000.
MULTIPLICATION WITH NUMBERS IN DECIMAL FORM.
hundreds. ten. singular. tenth. hundredth.
So what happens when a number in decimal form is multiplied by 10, 100 or 1000?
We look at an example, 100 * 1.45.
When you multiply a number by 100, all digits get a 100 times greater value.
In the example, the digit 1 moves from the ones position to the hundreds position.
You move the numbers as many steps to the left as there are zeros in 100.
