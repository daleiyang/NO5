PREFIX. The words deci, centi and milli are called prefixes.

Deci betyder tiondel, centi betyder hundradel och milli betyder tusendel. 
Deci means tenth, centi means hundredth and milli means thousandth.
Deci 表示十分之一，centi 表示百分之一，milli 表示千分之一。

En decimeter är lika med en tiondels meter, 
A decimeter is equal to one-tenth of a meter,

a centimeter equals one hundredth of a meter and a millimeter equals one thousandth of a meter.
