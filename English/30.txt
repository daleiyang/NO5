Crime doesn't pay.
犯罪弊大于利（得不偿失）

Sofie and Karim are in the newsagent's after sports day.
运动会结束后，“索菲”和“卡里姆”在报亭里。

They're discussing what sweets to buy.
他们在讨论买什么糖果。

I want some chocolate.
我想吃巧克力

Yeah, that sounds good. I'd like some chewing gum and a couple of gobstoppers, too.
听起来不错 我还想要一些口香糖和几个圆硬糖(颌骨破坏者)。

In that case I'll get some jelly beans. 
这样的话，我就去买些糖豆。

And I can still afford a bag of marshmallows. 
我还买得起一袋棉花糖。

And maybe a packet of salt & vinegar crisps.
也许还有一包盐醋薯片。

I don't think crisps are worth the money. 
我觉得薯片不值这个价。

Lollipops are cheaper, and they last longer.
棒棒糖更便宜，而且持续时间更长。

Do you really want gobstoppers and lollipops? They're almost the same.
你真的想要圆硬糖和棒棒糖吗？它们几乎是一样的。

Hmm, good thinking. Gobstoppers are more expensive, so...
嗯，好主意。圆硬糖更贵，所以...

Shopkeeper. Oy, come back, you!
店主。 喂，你回来！

Sofie and Karim turn around and see an older girl push past them.
索菲和卡里姆转过身来，看到一个年长一些的女孩从他们身边走过，还推了他们一把。

When she opens the door and runs outside something falls to the ground. 
当她打开门跑出去时，有东西掉在了地上。

The girl quickly disappears.
女孩很快就消失了。
