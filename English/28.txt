Netball! What is netball?
无挡板篮球 什么是无挡板篮球？

Netball is a fast and energetic sport that is usually played by women. 
无挡板篮球是一项快速而充满活力的运动，通常由女性进行。

Once upon a time, it was called women's basketball.
从前，它被称为女子篮球。

In the game, you run, jump, catch and throw the ball, but you can only bounce it once. 
在游戏中，你可以奔跑、跳跃、接球和扔球，但只能弹跳一次。

You score a goal when you throw the ball into the net.
当你把球扔进球网时，你就进球了。

How do you play netball?
您是如何打网球的？

There are seven people in a netball team, 
一个网球队有七个人，

but each player can only move in a small part of the netball court. 
但每个球员只能在网球场的一小部分活动。

You pass the ball to your team members by throwing it. 
您通过投掷将球传给您的团队成员。

The other team tries to stop — or block - you from getting the ball. 
对方试图阻止（或阻挡）你拿球。

The team that scores the most goals is the winner.
进球最多的队伍获胜。

What about the ball? Do you play with a basketball?
球呢？你玩篮球吗？

No, you play netball with a smaller and softer ball. 
不，你打网球用的是更小更软的球。

It's more like a football than a basketball.
它更像足球，而不是篮球。

How popular is the game? It's very popular! 
游戏有多受欢迎？非常受欢迎！

More than 20 million people enjoy playing netball in over 70 different countries. 
在 70 多个国家中，有 2000 多万人喜欢打无挡板篮球。

It is one of the top sports in Australia, New Zealand, Britain, the West Indies and Sri Lanka. 
它是澳大利亚、新西兰、英国、西印度群岛和斯里兰卡的顶级运动之一。

In fact, it became an Olympic sport in 1995.
事实上，它于 1995 年成为奥林匹克运动项目。
