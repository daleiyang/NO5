P 48

An outing to Stonehenge.
巨石阵之旅。

Lee and his class have just visited Stonehenge.
Lee和他的同学们刚刚参观了巨石阵。

They take their seats on the coach and wait for the teacher to arrive.
他们在大巴上就座，等待老师到来。

What do you think, then?
你觉得怎么样？

What an experience that was! Stonehenge is much bigger than I imagined.
这真是一次难忘的经历！巨石柱比我想象的要大得多。

Yeah, it’s more impressive than I thought, too. I'm glad I took some pictures.
是啊，它也比我想象的更令人印象深刻。很高兴我拍了些照片。

What about you, Lee? You're very quiet.
你呢, Lee? 你很安静。

I don’t know what to say.
我不知道该说什么。

I know what you mean. It takes your breath away, doesn’t it?
我知道你的意思。 它是令人叹为观止的，不是吗？

No, you don’t understand. I can’t think of a way to describe how bad it was.
不，你不明白。 我不知道该怎么形容那有多糟糕。

What? Are you serious?
什么？你说真的？

Totally. We spent most of the morning on a boiling-hot, tiny, noisy coach. 
当然 我们花了大半个上午的时间 坐在又热又小又吵的长途汽车上。

When we finally arrived I was tired, sweaty and I felt sick.
当我们终于到达目的地时，我又累又汗，还觉得恶心。

And what did we see after all that? A load of old stones.
然后我们看到了什么？一堆旧石头。

A load of old stones? Stonehenge is thousands of years old.
一堆古老的石头？巨石阵已经有几千年的历史了。

And we have no idea why the stones are here, or how they got here.
我们不知道这些石头为什么会在这里 也不知道它们是怎么来的。

P 49

Right. I read that you can only find this kind of stone in Wales.
是的。我听说只有在"威尔士"才能找到这种石头。

That's miles away. So how did people transport these huge stones all the way here?
那可是很远的地方 那么人们是怎么把这些巨大的石头一路运到这里的呢？

That's a good question. Why would anyone dump lots of rocks in the most boring place on Earth?
这是个好问题。 为什么会有人在地球上最无聊的地方倾倒大量的石头呢？

You are incredible, Lee. This is the countryside. It's beautiful!
你真不可思议，Lee。 这就是乡村。 太美了！

Um, yeah, you're right. Hills, more hills, fields, grass...
嗯，是的，你说得对。 山丘、 更多的山丘、 田野、 草地......

Oh, and a few trees in the distance. A fascinating place.
哦，远处还有几棵树。 真是个迷人的地方

Don't be sarcastic, Lee. Anyway, it's so peaceful. Not like Brighton.
别挖苦我了，Lee。 总之，这里很宁静。 不像“布莱顿”。

There are hundreds of tourists that get in your way, and there's a shop that sells horrible souvenirs. 
有成百上千的游客挡住你的去路， 还有一家卖恐怖纪念品的商店 

And, if you're stupid or hungry enough, you can buy overpriced food.
如果你够傻够饿， 你还能买到高价食物。

It's exactly like Brighton.
跟布莱顿一模一样。

God, Lee, you're so depressing. 
天啊，李，你真让人沮丧。

I'm not as depressing as this place.
我可没这里这么郁闷。
