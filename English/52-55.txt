What are YOU doing? It is time to save our planet!
您在做什么？是时候拯救我们的地球了！

The earth is getting hotter and hotter. We call this global warming.
地球正在变得越来越热。我们称之为全球变暖。

We humans burn lots of fossil fuels like oil, 
我们人类燃烧大量化石燃料，如石油、

coal and petrol and this makes the air around us much hotter. 
煤和汽油等化石燃料，这使得我们周围的空气变得更热。

Factories, cars and planes all add to the air and water pollution — and this also leads to global warming. 
工厂、汽车和飞机都加剧了空气和水污染，这也导致了全球变暖。

At the same time, we cut down or burn millions of trees every year, which makes the air warmer.
与此同时，我们每年都要砍伐或焚烧数百万棵树木，这也使空气变得更加温暖。

Global warming means extreme weather, flooding and melting ice. 
全球变暖意味着极端天气、洪水和冰雪融化。

This affects many animals, and some of them are becoming extinct.
这影响到许多动物，其中一些正在灭绝。

Here are some of the things YOU can do to help the Earth:
以下是您可以做的一些帮助地球的事情：

Save energy at home and school by turning off lights.
在家里和学校关灯节能。 

Don't leave your TV or computer on stand-by either.
也不要让电视或电脑处于待机状态。

Ban plastic bags from your home. Use a cloth bag instead.
禁止在家中使用塑料袋。用布袋代替。

Use public transport more often, or walk or cycle to school.
多使用公共交通工具，或步行或骑自行车上学。

Recycle paper material, aluminium cans, glass, plastic bottles and metal.
回收纸张、铝罐、玻璃、塑料瓶和金属。

Consume less and save the earth's resources.
减少消费，节约地球资源。

Buy second-hand clothes, electronic gadgets, bicycles and so on.
购买二手衣物、电子产品、自行车等。

Save water! Shower instead of taking a bath.
节约用水！用淋浴代替洗澡。

Turn off the tap when you are brushing your teeth.
刷牙时关掉水龙头。

Show your commitment and...Adopt an endangered animal today!
表明您的承诺...今天就领养一只濒危动物！
