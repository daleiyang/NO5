P 42

Babysitting blues.
保姆忧郁症

I was very angry with my family yesterday evening.
昨天晚上，我对家人非常生气。

Sometimes I think they are just a bunch of idiots!
有时我觉得他们就是一群白痴！

It started off when I said I wanted to play the double bass. 
一开始，我说我想弹低音提琴。

My brother started laughing and teasing me.
哥哥开始笑着取笑我。

"Are you going to learn to play the double bass? It's twice as big as you!”
"你要学低音提琴吗？它比你大一倍呢！"

"What about learning to play the cello, Mary?” my mom asked.
"学大提琴怎么样，'玛丽'？"妈妈问。

"Or the violin?” said Dad. ”Argh! Not the violin!” shouted Mom.
"还是小提琴？"爸爸说。

"还是小提琴？"爸爸说。"啊！不要小提琴！"妈妈喊道。

"Remember when Diego had violin lessons? It sounded like a cat crying...”
"还记得'迪亚哥'上小提琴课的时候吗？听起来就像猫在哭......"

"Anyway," continued Mom. "You already have a guitar. Why don't you take guitar lessons?”
"总之，"妈妈接着说。"你已经有一把吉他了。你为什么不学吉他呢？

They just don't get it. 
他们就是不明白。

I don't want to learn an instrument I have to blow in, like the flute or trumpet. 
我不想学一种必须吹奏的乐器，比如长笛或小号。

And I don't want to learn to strum my guitar because I can already do that.
我不想学弹吉他，因为我已经会弹了。

Actually, it would be kind of cool to learn how to play the synthesizer. 
事实上，如果能学会如何演奏合成器，那将是一件很酷的事情。

Then I could make up all kinds of new sounds.
然后，我就可以创造出各种新的声音。

Anyway, my evening got worse. I had plans, big plans.
总之，我的夜晚变得更糟了。我有计划，很大的计划。

But my parents ruined my whole evening.
但我的父母毁了我的整个夜晚。

"Remember that you're going to babysit Rosie tonight?” my Dad said. 
"还记得你今晚要照看'罗西'吗？"爸爸说。

Then they went out to the movies and left me with Rosie, who started crying her eyes out.
然后他们出去看电影，把我和'罗西'留在一起，'罗西'开始哭得死去活来。

You know what?  I think I'm going to learn to play the bagpipes...as revenge on everyone. 
你知道吗？ 我想我要学会吹风笛......作为对所有人的报复。

That will teach them!
这将教会他们！
