Bonfire Night. SAFETY RULES.
Don't stand too near the bonfire.
Don't put fireworks in the bonfire or near it.
Don't throw fireworks at anyone.
Don't play with fireworks or matches.
Don't go near a firework that has been lit. 
It can still explode. Keep pets indoors.
Ask an adult for help when lighting fireworks or sparklers.
