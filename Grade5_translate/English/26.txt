A sporting chance.
一次运动的机会

Today was the worst Bonfire Night ever. 
今天是有史以来最糟糕的篝火之夜。

Cloudy, unbelievably chilly... and it was sports day.
阴云密布，寒风刺骨......而且今天是运动会。

After morning assembly we all walked to the sports centre.
晨会结束后，我们步行前往体育中心。

At the entrance Mrs Atkins told us to be quiet.
在入口处，Atkins夫人让我们保持安静。

In a moment I'll divide you up into four groups - red, yellow, green and blue. 
等一下我会把你们分成红黄绿蓝四组。

We'll then make our way into the hall and begin the first event: netball.
然后我们进入大厅，开始第一个项目：无挡板篮球。

I can't stand netball. I don't understand the rules and I can't catch. 
我受不了无挡板篮球。 我不懂规则，也接不到球。

I pretended that I hurt my wrist, 
我假装手腕受伤，

and tried to spend as much time as possible on the sidelines.
尽量在场边待着。

My yellow team did well, though, despite my ”injury”. 
尽管我“受伤”了，但我的黄队还是表现出色。

We lost against the blue team in the final.
我们在决赛中输给了蓝队。

Next was table tennis. 
接下来是乒乓球。

I'm a wicked player, so I knew I could go far.
我是个厉害的选手，所以我知道我能走得更远。

I met a girl from 5B - Mary something — and she was useless. 
我遇到了一个来自 5B 的女孩--玛丽什么的--她很没用。

But I lost! She slaughtered me, and I was furious. 
但我输了！她把我打得落花流水，我气疯了。

Then she said "Thanks for the game. Nice try!”
然后她说："谢谢你的游戏。干得好！"

I almost hit the roof. I was livid. I couldn't eat my packed lunch.
我几乎要发飙了。 我气疯了。 我吃不下打包好的午餐。
