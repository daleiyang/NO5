turning point

When the protagonist has encountered the same thing several times the story usually turns. 
This is called a turning point.
What is the turning point in your horror story?

One evening when the sisters are brushing their teeth, the ghost jumps out of the mirror and grabs one of the sisters.

But the other sister manages to grab the aunt and flush her down the toilet.

The ending

Horror stories often end with a twist or an open ending where the scare is not quite gone.
