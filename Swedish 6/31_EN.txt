Objectives

Reading

I can:
distinguish the structure and features of a comparative factual text,
search for similarities and differences between events/things, 
identify comparison words that signal similarities and differences between events/things.

Writing

I can:
gather important facts about two phenomena/things,
sort facts according to similarities and differences,
write a comparative factual text with an introduction, similarities, differences and a summary conclusion,
use comparison words to signal similarities and differences,
write and spell correctly.

Speaking

I can:
plan and carry out a comparative study,
orally present a survey,
clearly show similarities and differences between different products,
include the three parts of the report: introduction, content and summary with final judgment.
