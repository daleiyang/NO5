In order to feel as good as possible, there are a number of factors that are important: Physical health.
We need movement and physical activity to feel good.
Through training you become stronger, get better mobility and fitness.
The skeleton is strengthened and osteoporosis is prevented.
The immune system is strengthened if you exercise properly.
Everyday exercise is what we do on a daily basis without changing to move.
e.g. cycle, walk, walk the dog or take the stairs.
Exercise builds self-confidence. 
By exercising, you increase your fitness and the better your fitness, the more you can cope. 
Fitness is perishable, so it is important to exercise regularly.
ideally two to three times a week.
When you are sick, you should not exercise because it is dangerous for your body.
