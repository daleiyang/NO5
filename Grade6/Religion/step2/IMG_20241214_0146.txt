FREJA  
Freja var fruktbarhetsgudinna.  
Freja was the fertility goddess.  
芙莱雅是丰收女神。

Hon var mycket vacker.  
She was very beautiful.  
她非常美丽。

Jättarna försökte ofta röva bort henne.  
The giants often tried to kidnap her.  
巨人常常试图绑架她。

Till Freja bad människorna när de ville ha barn.  
People prayed to Freja when they wanted children.  
人们在想要孩子时向芙莱雅祈祷。

LOKE  
Loke var egentligen släkt med jättarna men tillhörde asagudarna ändå.  
Loke was actually related to the giants but still belonged to the Norse gods.  
洛基实际上与巨人有亲戚关系，但仍然属于北欧诸神。

Han var en listig gud som ofta skapade problem för de andra gudarna.  
He was a cunning god who often caused problems for the other gods.  
他是一个狡猾的神，经常给其他神带来麻烦。

Men Loke hjälpte också till många gånger.  
But Loke also helped many times.  
但洛基也帮助过很多次。

IDUN  
Idun var ungdomens gudinna.  
Idun was the goddess of youth.  
伊登是青春女神。

Hon hade äpplen som höll asagudarna unga.  
She had apples that kept the Norse gods young.  
她有使北欧诸神保持年轻的苹果。

HEL  
Gudinnan Hel var dotter till Loke.  
The goddess Hel was Loke's daughter.  
女神赫尔是洛基的女儿。

Hon styrde i underjorden.  
She ruled in the underworld.  
她在冥界统治。

Dit kom människorna efter döden.  
There people came after death.  
人们死后去那里。

Orden helvete och det engelska ordet hell kommer från gudinnan Hel.  
The words helvete and the English word hell come from the goddess Hel.  
单词helvete和英文单词hell都来源于女神赫尔。
