God gave the Jews rules they should follow.
When Moses walked through the desert on his way from Egypt to Canaan, something special happened.
One day when he climbed a mountain, he received ten rules, the Ten Commandments, from God.
The Jews should always follow these commandments.
The commandments are also important in Christianity.
On the next page you can read more about them.
On this mountain, Moses met God.
It is called Mount Sinai and is located in the Sinai Desert in Egypt.
It was there that he received the Ten Commandments.
