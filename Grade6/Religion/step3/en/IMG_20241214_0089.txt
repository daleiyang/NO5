SUMMARY
Muslims believe in a single God who in Arabic is called Allah.
Muhammad is the greatest prophet, and he lived between the years 570 and 632.
The place where Muslims celebrate worship is called a mosque.
The five pillars tell how a Muslim should live.
A Muslim should pray five times a day.
The Quran is Islam's holy book. According to Muslims, it contains the word of God.
The biggest Muslim holiday is called the festival of breaking the fast.
The two largest groups are Sunni Muslims and Shia Muslims.
