Krishna is a popular god
Krishna is one of the most popular gods in Hinduism.
Some Hindus believe that Krishna is the only god that exists.
There are many stories about Krishna.
It is told, among other things, how he saves the world, how he participates in a war, and how he, as a young flute-playing shepherd, plays with some shepherdesses.
Krishna is known for his flute playing and his songs.
People who worship him sing in his honor.
