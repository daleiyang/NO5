These nuns live in Bangkok.
All Buddhist nuns and monks have shaved heads.
This is to show that they live apart from the rest of the world.
There are two main groups
There are several different groups but the two most important are:
1. Mahayana (the great vehicle).
There one prays to Buddha as a god.
One also prays to other gods and spirits.
2. Theravada (the old teaching).
There one sees Buddha as an important person who helps humans understand life.
But he is not a god to whom one prays.
