In Christianity, it is an important rule that one should "love your neighbor as yourself."
It means that you should care for all your fellow humans as much as you care for yourself.

A day off
In Judaism, the Sabbath is important.
One day a week, people should rest from their jobs, as God did when he had finished creating the world.
Even if one wants to work, one should not, if one believes in God and wants to follow the religion.
