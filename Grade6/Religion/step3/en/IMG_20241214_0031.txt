Christianity
Different Christian groups
There are more than 2 billion Christian people in the world, that is more than 2,000 million people.
This makes Christianity the world's largest religion.
Christians live in different countries and have different traditions and history.
Therefore, many Christian groups have formed.
The largest groups are called Catholics, Protestants, and Orthodox.
Catholics
The largest group of Christians is called Catholics, and they belong to the Catholic Church.
Over 1,000 million people are Catholics.
The Catholic Church is present in almost all countries.
The highest leader of the Catholic Church is the Pope.
He lives in Italy, in his own small state in the city of Rome.
It's called Vatican City.
The Pope addresses the thousands of Catholics gathered in the open space of Vatican City.
