The god Ganesha is easy to recognize because he has an elephant head.
He is the god of learning, and therefore schoolchildren pray to him to succeed in their studies.
Ganesha is the god of schoolchildren.
Ganesha is not only a god who protects.
He is also the god of learning.
Since he is the god of learning, he is extra important for all schoolchildren.
They pray to him to succeed in their studies.
Ganesha has only one tusk.
This is because according to a story, he broke off the other tusk and used it as a pen to write down one of the important books that Hindus have.
Learning means knowledge.
