What is religion?
In this book, we will talk about different religions.
The five largest religions in the world are called Christianity, Judaism, Islam, Hinduism, and Buddhism.
They are called world religions.
At the beginning of each chapter, we visit a family's home.
Then, you will read some important stories and learn more about what is celebrated and believed in each religion.
Religions Tell About Life
A religion helps people understand how the world and life are.
It tells its view on how the earth and humans came to be.
It also explains how we humans should treat each other.
But above all, religions are about faith in God, gods, or higher powers.
