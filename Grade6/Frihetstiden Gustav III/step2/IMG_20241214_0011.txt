"Ge rum vid roddartrappan! undan birfilare, skoputsare, tullsnokar och matroser!"  
"Make way at the rowing stairs! Clear the way for boatmen, shoeshiners, customs officers and sailors!" 
“在划船梯上让路！让开船夫、擦鞋匠、海关官员和水手！”

Så sjöng Bellman om 1700-talets Stockholm.  
Thus sang Bellman about 18th century Stockholm.  
贝尔曼这样唱18世纪的斯德哥尔摩。

I långa rader låg fartygen längs Skeppsbron och lossade sin last.  
In long rows the ships lay along Skeppsbron and unloaded their cargo.  
在长长的队列中，船只沿着Skeppsbron停泊并卸下货物。

Hamnarbetarna sprang om varandra med sina bördor, det luktade tjära, kaffe, kryddor och nysågat virke.  
The dock workers raced past each other with their burdens, it smelled of tar, coffee, spices, and freshly cut timber.  
码头工人们带着负担争先恐后，闻到焦油、咖啡、香料和新锯木材的味道。

I dag luktar Skeppsbron mest avgaser.  
Today Skeppsbron mostly smells of exhaust fumes.  
今天，Skeppsbron大多闻起来像废气。
