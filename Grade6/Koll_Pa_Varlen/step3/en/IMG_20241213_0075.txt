The pattern for the jeans is designed in the USA.
The zippers are manufactured in Turkey or Lithuania from brass from Japan.
The polyester fiber that becomes thread is manufactured in Japan from oil from Saudi Arabia.
The pockets are sewn in factories in Pakistan.
The rivets are manufactured in Germany from copper from Namibia or Australia.
From different parts of the world, the parts are sent to a textile factory in China, India, or Pakistan.
There, the parts are sewn together into jeans that are sent out all over the world.
