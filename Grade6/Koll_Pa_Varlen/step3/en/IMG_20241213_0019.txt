Landslides
Areas that are steep and have loose soil layers can be affected by landslides.
Then large amounts of soil are set in motion.
It can be caused by heavy rain or earthquakes.
A landslide can change an area quickly.
Large landslides can drag down entire houses and roads.
A canyon is a large and very deep valley.
The Grand Canyon in the USA is probably the world's most famous canyon.
At the bottom of the Grand Canyon flows the Colorado River.
The flowing water has, over a long time, carved into the rock and formed this enormous valley.
