Karoliner
Carolingians
卡洛林王朝

Den karolinska tidens indelningsverk var till för att lättare få fram yrkessoldater. 
The division system of the Carolingian period was designed to make it easier to produce professional soldiers. 
卡洛林王朝时期的分封制旨在更容易地培养职业军人。

Bönderna delades in i rotar (grupper) med 2-5 gårdar i varje. 
Farmers were divided into rotaries (groups) with 2-5 farms in each. 
农民被划分为轮牧（组），每个轮牧组有 2-5 个农场。

Varje rote skulle försörja en soldat. 
Each rota was to support one soldier. 
每个轮值农场供养一名士兵。

Soldaten fick ett torp för sin familj och hjälp med hö och ved. 
The soldier was given a croft for his family and help with hay and firewood. 
士兵可以得到一个庄园供其家庭居住，并得到干草和木柴方面的帮助。

Så slapp bönderna och deras söner själva gå ut i kriget.
This meant that farmers and their sons did not have to go to war themselves.
这意味着农民和他们的儿子不必亲自上战场。

Inga galna soldater!
No crazy soldiers!
没有疯狂的士兵

De män som bönderna i roten valde ut till soldater samlades till stora mönstringar. 
The men selected as soldiers by the peasants in the root area were gathered for large musters. 
被根区农民选为士兵的人被集中起来进行大集合。

Vid mönstringen kontrollerades de och skrevs in i listor. 
At the muster they were checked and entered on lists. 
在集合时，他们要接受检查并被列入名单。

Ofta var kungen med och godkände soldaterna. Om en man inte dög strök kungen över hans namn.
The king was often present and approved the soldiers. If a man was not suitable, the king crossed out his name.
国王经常出席并批准士兵。如果某个人不合适，国王就会划掉他的名字。

Någon gång skrev han ”Karlen är galen — kasseras”. 
Sometimes he wrote “The man is mad - discarded”. 
有时他会写上 “此人疯了--弃用”。

Soldaterna i Karl XII:s armé kallas karoliner. 
The soldiers in Charles XII's army are called Carolinians. 
查理十二世军队中的士兵被称为 “卡洛林人”。

En karolin skulle vara orädd, trogen, nykter, god kamrat och kunna sjunga visor och berätta sagor. 
A Carolinian had to be fearless, faithful, sober, a good comrade and able to sing songs and tell stories. 
卡洛林人必须无所畏惧、忠诚、清醒、是个好同志，还能唱歌和讲故事。

De utvalda soldaterna övades i att marschera och skjuta.
The selected soldiers were trained to march and shoot.
被选中的士兵要接受行军和射击训练。

Eftersom många soldater hade samma namn tog sig soldaterna efternamn som Rolig, Vass, Modig och Gås.
As many soldiers had the same name, the soldiers took on surnames such as Funny, Sharp, Brave and Goose.
由于许多士兵同名同姓，他们被冠以滑稽、犀利、勇敢和鹅等姓氏。

I soldattorpet brukade hela familjen bo i ett rum.
The whole family used to live in one room in the soldiers' tent.
全家人住在士兵帐篷的一个房间里。

Med många barn blev det trångt.
With many children, it got crowded.
孩子多了，就显得拥挤了。

This is a grenade case, a powder flask and a cartridge holder used by a Carolinian
这是一个手榴弹盒、一个火药瓶和一个卡洛林人使用的弹夹

Karolinen fick en uniform som brukade bestå av en blå rock, skinnbyxor och gula strumpor.
The Carolinian was given a uniform, which used to consist of a blue coat, leather pants and yellow socks.
卡洛林人的制服通常包括蓝色外套、皮裤和黄袜。

På ryggen hade han en ränsel(ryggsäck).
On his back he had a knapsack.
他背上背着一个背包。
