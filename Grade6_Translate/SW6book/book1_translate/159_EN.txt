Pashto 



One of the two main languages spoken in Afghanistan.



SHALWAR KAMIZ 



A long, wide shirt and loose-fitting trousers. 



Worn by both men and women. 



Men's are always plain with a breast pocket and side pocket. 



Women's have different colors and patterns, sometimes richly embroidered or beaded.



SOVIET UNION 



A union of communist-ruled countries, including Russia, which dissolved in 1989.



TALIBANES 



Muslim fundamentalists, members of the ruling party in Afghanistan between 1996 and 2001.



TosHAK 



A narrow mattress used instead of chairs and beds in many Afghan homes.
