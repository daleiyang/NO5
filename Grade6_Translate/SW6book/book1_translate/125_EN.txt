Parvana heard thuds and more screams.



Without thinking, she rushed up and looked at the window, 



but she could see nothing through the stained glass.



“What happens in your own home is every man's business,” said a voice behind her. 



She turned around and saw a man holding out an envelope.



“Forget that now and get on with your work.



I have a letter I want you to read.”



She had planned to tell her family about the incident that evening, but she never got the chance.



Instead, it was the family who had something to tell her.



“You'll never guess what it is,” her mother said.



“Nooria is getting married.”
