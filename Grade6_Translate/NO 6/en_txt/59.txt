Goals  
When you have worked with this chapter, you should  
be able to explain what an atom and a molecule are  
know what an element is and give examples  
know what a chemical compound is and give examples  
know that substances have different properties  
be able to talk about the carbon cycle in nature  
understand the human role in the carbon cycle  
know what humans can use different metals for  
be able to explain how to extract metals from ores  
know why some different substances are recycled.
