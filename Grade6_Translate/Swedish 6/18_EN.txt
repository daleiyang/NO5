Now it's your turn to present

You are now going to present your book.
For the report to be good, it should contain the following elements:

The mouth symbolizes that:

you will grab your listeners with an interesting introduction.

The full belly symbolizes that you are telling the story:

facts about the book, such as the title, author, illustrator and what type of book it is.

what the book is about.

who the important characters are in the book and if and how they develop.

where and when the book takes place

something about the language of the book.

The stars on the tail symbolize that:

you are concluding with a summary opinion.

you give the book a rating.
