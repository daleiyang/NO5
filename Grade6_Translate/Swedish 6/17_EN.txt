Interpreting the message and giving an opinion

In a presentation, it is interesting for the listeners to hear how you interpret the message of the book and what you think of the book.

What do you think the author wants to say with his book?

What do you think is good and less good in the book? Why do you think so?

Do you think the book is worth reading and would you recommend it to others? Why do you think so?
