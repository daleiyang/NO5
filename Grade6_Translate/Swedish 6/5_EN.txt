Objectives

Reading

I can:
Read a narrative text with fluency and good understanding
understand how authors build up descriptions of setting and characters
understand how writers use dialogues.

Speaking

I can:
plan and deliver an oral presentation of a book I have read
use pictures and/or digital media when giving a presentation
adapt my language to those listening.

Writing

I can: 
plan and write a horror short story with, threat/what scares, events, turning point and ending,
use descriptive language in my horror story so that descriptions of setting and characters are vivid,
using dialogues,
writing and spelling correctly.
