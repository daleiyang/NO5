Narrative text

If you've ever read a book that was so good you almost couldn't put it down, you've certainly had a reading experience.

But have you ever wondered how authors capture their readers?

In this chapter, you'll look at the tricks writers use to describe different settings and how they write about characters to make them come alive. 

You will use these techniques when you write your own chilling horror story at the end of the chapter.
